Before blog creation, I used to share my experience via Medium articles, here are some of them:

- [Confidential assets in EVM](https://medium.com/@oleg.fomenko2002/confidential-assets-in-evm-5e44c31e3088). Describes
  an approach presented in [Confidential assets paper](https://blockstream.com/bitcoin17-final41.pdf) to make blockchain
  transactions with hidden amounts written using Solidity language for EVM-compatible blockchains.
- [Solana program trustful upgrade](https://medium.com/@oleg.fomenko2002/solana-program-trustful-upgrade-e6733bff4581)
  explains how to manage upgrades of Solana programs in a secure way.
- [Rarimo: How to build a bridge?](https://medium.com/@oleg.fomenko2002/rarimo-how-to-build-a-bridge-6594e65cf214)
  observes the principles that have been used in [Rarimo](https://rarimo.com/) to build trustless bridge.
